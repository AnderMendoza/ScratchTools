ScratchTools

It's the perfect browser extension for Scratch!

The settings button is always on the Scratch homepage.

  • Follower count on projects
  • More specific text for closed profile comments
  • More tutorials on the ideas page
  • Message count on profiles
  • Easily propose projects to be featured
  • Quickly open projects in Turbowarp
  • Rounded corners on profile avatars and featured projects
  • Simply find your statistics in "My Stuff"
  • Links to projects in profile comments switch to title
  • Hide sprite watermark in the editor
  • Hover over project titles on profiles to show full title
  • Show * next to Scratch Team members in the forums
  • Hover over username in profile comments to show bio
  • Remove dots from background in code editor
  • Save forum posts as drafts and use them later
  • Replace the Scratch News with your recent projects
  • Put the ScratchTools Settings button in the footer
  • On project pages, automatically open links in a new tab
  • Ocular buttons for users in the forums
  • Slider for outline in the paint editor
  • Remove the icons for the block, paint, and sound editors
  • Show NFE status of any project
  • Unbold the forum post titles

As you can see, ScratchTools has a lot to offer, and it's constantly growing.
